About Me

Shahwat Hasnaine - Software Engineer

Born and raised in Chittagong. Shifted to Dhaka with my family in 2010. Currently studying undergrad at the Rajshahi University of Engineering Technology with BSc. in Electrical & Computer Engineering. While in university, actively took part in many programming competetion. Also participated in various photography-related and volunteer activities in the whole of my student life.

I always consider myself a passionate person. Whatever I did in my whole life, did with everything I have. Photography is my hobby and doing it since childhood. Coding is what earns me money, brings me happiness. I also started launching startups recently one by one with my friends and some of them are also succeeding in their own field by the grace of Almighty Allah.

"Allah has better plan for you than you"

I practice Islam with all my heart. I believe everyone has right to practice what he wants.
Whenever I feel down, I always remember the quote mentioned above and try to remain calm for the better result.
And you know what? It always pays me off.
